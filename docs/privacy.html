Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how MixMagic ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application.

2. Camera Permission

Our app uses camera permission exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Scanning QR codes and barcodes to identify cocktail ingredients
  • Capturing product information for your personal ingredient library

Important: We do not store any camera images on our servers. All camera data is processed exclusively locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

3. Bluetooth Permission

Bluetooth permission is used to connect to compatible kitchen scales so you can precisely measure cocktail ingredients.

4. Location Permission

Location permission is only required for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) functionality, as mandated by Android. We do not collect or store any location data.

5. Data Storage

All your cocktail recipes, ingredient lists, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Optionally, you can synchronize your data with our secure cloud service.

6. Data Transmission

We only transmit data that you explicitly authorize for synchronization. Camera or location data is never transmitted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Correct or delete your data
  • Restrict data processing
  • Export your data
